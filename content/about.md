<section class="about-intro">
<p>This site is where my politics, spirituality, disability, Jewishness, queerness, anger, tenderness, and late-night thoughts all get to exist in the same room. I write because silence has never suited me very well.</p>
</section>

<section class="belief-grid" aria-label="Core beliefs">
<article class="belief-card">
<span><i class="fa-solid fa-scale-balanced" aria-hidden="true"></i> Equality</span>
<h2>People should be free to be fully themselves.</h2>
<p>I oppose racism, homophobia, transphobia, misogyny, antisemitism, ableism, ageism, and every other form of bigotry that tries to make people smaller or less safe.</p>
</article>

<article class="belief-card">
<span><i class="fa-solid fa-coins" aria-hidden="true"></i> Capitalism</span>
<h2>Systems that protect wealth over people deserve scrutiny.</h2>
<p>I do not support an economy designed to keep the wealthy wealthy and the poor poor while treating survival as something people must earn through exhaustion.</p>
</article>

<article class="belief-card">
<span><i class="fa-solid fa-dove" aria-hidden="true"></i> Peace</span>
<h2>War should never be treated as inevitable.</h2>
<p>I oppose the military industrial complex, endless war, foreign intervention for profit or power, and the idea that peace can be built at the end of a missile.</p>
</article>

<article class="belief-card">
<span><i class="fa-solid fa-chalkboard-user" aria-hidden="true"></i> Education</span>
<h2>Teachers and students deserve better.</h2>
<p>Education should be funded, humane, and liberating. Teachers deserve real wages, real support, and a system that values learning over standardized test performance.</p>
</article>
</section>

<section class="about-note">
<h2>What You Will Find Here</h2>
<p>Expect personal essays, political frustration, disability honesty, spiritual reflection, music devotion, occasional chaos, and a stubborn belief that the open web is still worth tending.</p>
</section>
