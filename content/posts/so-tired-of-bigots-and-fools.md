I watched a video today of overgrown man-child Elon Musk—pretty sure it was on Fox News—whining about people being mad at Tesla because of the dumb shit _he’s_ doing.

https://www.youtube.com/embed/JIxv7_L5wEM

Of course, he has absolutely zero self-awareness or even a shred of humanity in his overstuffed, fridge-shaped frame—like a malfunctioning smart appliance desperately trying to prove it’s human. So instead of reflecting, he lashes out at Governor Walz and asks, _“Who derives joy from this?”_—referring to Tesla hemorrhaging money.

To which I say: _I derive much joy from this!_

For _years_, right-wingers have screamed _“Go woke, go broke!”_ and _loved_ boycotts—gleefully sticking it to any company that didn’t fall in line with their backwards-ass worldview. But the moment it’s _“Go Fash, Lose Cash,”_ suddenly, they’re crying foul. _How very dare you!_

Funny how that works.

Now, this might not be the deepest or smartest take in the world, but it’s what’s on my mind: We’re watching people get deported (or face deportation attempts) for allegedly not showing the _proper_ deference to this country. And yet—let’s be real—Elon Musk is not from America. He came here just like the very people they’re trying to kick out.

What’s their crime? Not falling in lockstep with the _“Israel can do no wrong”_ mentality. And now, they’re being targeted. Meanwhile, this bloated changeling actively works to dismantle American democracy—an act that, in my opinion, borders on war or treason—but _he_ gets to stay?

Students and others exercised their _legal_ right to association and protest, and _they’re_ the ones facing expulsion? Make it make sense.

And what’s worse—no one on the right gives a shit, because _“owning the libs”_ is apparently worth more to them than democracy itself. Repugnant.

You can have your debates about how money should be spent, whether programs need reform, and the best ways to implement policy. Fine. But at the bare fucking minimum, you should be able to look at what’s happening and say: _This is not normal. This is not okay._

Our so-called _Greatest Generation_ fought—and some paid the ultimate price—to keep fascism _off_ our shores. The very _least_ we can do is honor their sacrifice by refusing to let it take root now.

But that’s not what’s happening.

And it makes me so fucking sick.
