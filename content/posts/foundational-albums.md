## A Fever you can't sweat out 
### Panic! At the Disco

Music truly is at the center of who I am. And at that core, these 15  albums were on CONSTANT repeat (much to the dismay of my family) during  my High-school years (2004-2007). There were a smattering of other  albums, but these were my core. If you were to say to me I could only  choose 15 albums to listen to for the rest of my life, I could live with  these 15.
